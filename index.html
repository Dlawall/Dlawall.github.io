Hi There!

My Name is Daniel Lawall.

I'm a third year multi-platform journalism major at the University of Maryland in College Park, Md., and an currently covering the Maryland varsity baseball team for Vox Media's Testudo Times. Through my experience here at UMD I have strengthened my written and oral communication skills, reporting prowess, and my ability to work under pressure and deadlines.

I'm an avid New York Yankees fan, I love to spend time outdoors and I'm an avid fan of all genres of music.
